About AGCC Foundation

AGCC Foundation is dedicated to empowering girls and young women through education, leadership development, mentorship, and community engagement.

We believe that every girl deserves equal opportunities to learn, grow, and contribute positively to society.

Our Mission

To educate, empower, and elevate girls through access to learning, leadership opportunities, mentorship, and community support.

Our Vision

A world where every girl has the confidence, opportunity, and resources needed to achieve her goals and become a leader.

Our Values

Education

Promoting learning and personal growth.

Equality

Supporting equal opportunities for all girls.

Leadership

Developing future leaders and changemakers.
